Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about HotAnime

How do I watch anime trailers on HotAnime?

Watching trailers is easy! Just browse our collection, click on any anime you're interested in, and hit the play button on the trailer. Make sure you have a stable internet connection for the best experience. If you're having trouble, try refreshing the page or checking your browser settings to ensure autoplay is enabled.

How often is the upcoming anime list updated?

Our upcoming anime list is updated daily with the latest information from studios and official sources. We monitor announcements continuously to bring you the most accurate release dates and information. For real-time updates, you can enable notifications in your account settings.

Can I interact with other anime fans on HotAnime?

Not yet!

Why can't I find a specific anime trailer?

While we strive to have the most comprehensive collection, some trailers may be unavailable due to regional restrictions or licensing issues. If you can't find a specific trailer, try using our search function with different keywords, or contact our support team who may be able to help locate it or add it to our database.

How do I report an issue or inappropriate content?

We take content quality and community standards seriously. To report an issue, click the "Report" button near the content in question, or contact our support team directly through the form below. Please provide as many details as possible to help us address your concern quickly.
